Zephyr become an annual workshop on Aerospace Education and Research at IIT Bombay. It aims to provide a platform for students, scientists, professors and the industry to come together, showcase their work and exchange ideas. A national platform for the confluence of aerospace students, enthusiasts, astronauts and companies. It caters to the layman who is fascinated by flying crafts as to the expert who makes fascinating crafts. With challenging competitions, interactive workshops, glamorous and informative exhibitions, fascinating airshows, panel debates and the coolest lectures by the role models in the field of Aerospace and Aviation. Guest lectures, panel debates, exhibitions. Zephyr has it all!
