ABOUT

This two-round competition is open to all engineering students who are interested in showcasing their design and problem-solving skills. Participants will be required to purchase the Pluto drone, which will serve as the platform for this competition.

The task is to design and implement a dropping mechanism that is capable of releasing payload at the desired location and altitude, while also ensuring the stability and control of the drone. The final solution will be evaluated based on the accuracy of payload release, stability of the drone during release process, user-friendliness and ease of operation, integration with the Pluto drone, and overall functionality and reliability.

Requirements:

1. The dropping mechanism must be capable of releasing payload at the desired location and altitude.

2. The mechanism must maintain the stability and control of the Pluto drone during the release process

3. The mechanism must be user-friendly and easy to operate

4. The mechanism must take into account weight restrictions and power supply limitations of the Pluto 1.2 Drone

5. The mechanism must perform consistently and reliably
